Flowers, recycled plastic bottles, and delivery from our volunteers are the ingredients. We run on love, no money needed :)

Our volunteers collect and recycle gently used flowers from stores and events for the benefit of homeless shelters, women's shelters, and other social service outreach facilities.

Flower Power runs on the kindness and generosity of our donors and volunteers.
